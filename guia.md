Create a modern, responsive landing page for a nonprofit organization (NGO) called “Fundación Alimentando Sonrisas (FAS)” based in Bolivia.

The design must feel emotional, human, warm, and trustworthy — not corporate. Focus on storytelling and user connection. The main goal is to drive donations and participation.

----------------------------------
NAVIGATION MENU
----------------------------------

Top navbar:

- Inicio
- Quiénes somos
- Cómo ayudamos (with dropdown submenu)
- Comunidades
- Nuestro equipo
- Participa
- Apóyanos (highlighted as primary button)
- Contacto

Dropdown submenu under “Cómo ayudamos”:
- Alimentación
- Salud
- Educación
- Emprendimiento

Navbar must be sticky and responsive (mobile collapsible menu).

----------------------------------
VISUAL DESIGN (VERY IMPORTANT)
----------------------------------

- Use a light color palette
- Primary color: soft green (IMPORTANT)

Suggested green tones:
- #6DBE7B (main soft green)
- #A8D5BA (lighter green)
- #E8F5E9 (very light background green)

- Secondary colors:
  - white (#FFFFFF)
  - light beige / warm neutral tones

- Avoid dark, saturated, or aggressive colors

- Use:
  - clean layout
  - lots of whitespace
  - rounded cards
  - soft shadows

- Typography:
  - friendly, modern, highly readable

The overall feeling should be:
“natural, hopeful, and human-centered”

----------------------------------
SCROLL & INTERACTION EFFECTS
----------------------------------

Use subtle scroll-based effects. Do NOT overload the page.

Include:

- Smooth Scroll (soft transitions between sections)

- Fade / Reveal Animations:
  Elements fade-in or slide-up when entering viewport

- Subtle Parallax Effect:
  Only in hero or one section (very light)

- Sticky Navbar:
  Navbar remains fixed at top

- Light Scroll-driven animations:
  Small movement tied to scroll

Optional (minimal use):
- Slight scale on hover
- Soft card animations

Avoid:
- heavy motion
- complex rotations
- animation overload

----------------------------------
HERO SECTION (WITH SLIDER)
----------------------------------

Create a hero section with an image slider (carousel):

Each slide must include:
- Background image (real community, children, rural Bolivia)
- Overlay gradient for readability
- Title (emotional)
- Short description
- CTA buttons:
  - “Apóyanos”
  - “Participa”

Slider behavior:
- Auto-play (slow, smooth)
- Manual navigation (arrows + dots)
- Smooth transitions (fade or slide)
- Optional subtle parallax on background

Example slides:

Slide 1:
“Ayudamos a niños a tener un mejor futuro”

Slide 2:
“Llevamos alimentación y apoyo a comunidades Guaraníes”

Slide 3:
“Juntos podemos generar nuevas oportunidades”

----------------------------------
LANDING PAGE STRUCTURE
----------------------------------

1. PROBLEM SECTION
- Short emotional explanation:
  - poverty
  - lack of access to food and education
- No statistics

----------------------------------

2. HOW WE HELP (PROGRAMS)
- 4 cards:
  - Alimentación
  - Salud
  - Educación
  - Emprendimiento
- Each:
  - icon
  - short text
  - subtle hover animation

----------------------------------

3. COMMUNITIES SECTION
- Show:
  - Tentayape
  - Sotos
  - Ytapembia
  - Pentirenda
  - Ibabirante
- Include images + short descriptions
- Use soft reveal animations

----------------------------------

4. STORY / TESTIMONIAL
- Emotional story (child, family, or team)
- Focus on human connection

----------------------------------

5. HOW TO HELP

Split layout:

A. Donar
- QR placeholder
- CTA: “Apóyanos ahora”

B. Participar
- Volunteer / help
- CTA: “Quiero participar”

----------------------------------

6. TRUST SECTION
- Team preview
- Mission summary
- Reinforce credibility

----------------------------------

7. FINAL CTA
- Emotional message
- Button: “Apóyanos”

----------------------------------

8. FOOTER
- Contact info
- WhatsApp
- Social links
- Navigation links

----------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES
----------------------------------

- Do NOT include statistics or impact numbers
- Focus on storytelling and emotion
- Keep text concise
- Use real-life imagery style
- Maintain performance (lightweight animations only)
